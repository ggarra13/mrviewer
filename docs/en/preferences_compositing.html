Compositing Preferences

The Compositing Preferences tab allows you to configure how image/video compositing works.

Resize Background Image

By default, the background image is scaled to fit the foreground image's size. Turning this off will leave the background image in its original size.

Blend Mode

OpenGL blend compositing mode.

Traditional:

SC x SA + DC x ( 1 - SA)


Premult:

SC + DC x ( 1 - SA )

where SC is source color, SA is source alpha, and DC is destination color.

The Gamma variations go around the gamma by unpremult and premult the channels after the gamma operation.
